Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address and account credentials when you register, as well as any expense data you choose to log including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Your expense data is used to generate summaries and insights about your spending patterns. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal financial data with third parties. We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure, but these providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and data protection obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can export or delete all your expense data through your account settings. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to addressing any concerns you may have and will respond promptly to any inquiries regarding your privacy.
